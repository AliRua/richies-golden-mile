Richie's Golden Mile


RGM is an annual open-water swim from Sherkin Island to Baltimore harbour. Covering a distance of one nautical mile, it is the perfect swim-meet for your calander whether you're just beginning your openwater adventure or are a seasoned triathlete looking to rack up your practice.

Location


Situated on the Teddy Bears toes of Ireland in Baltimore, West Cork, you'll find this is one of the most beautiful and idyllic settings for a swim along the Wild Atlantic Way. Hosted late into the balmy month of June against the backdrop of this cosy fishing village, the swim itself - and of course the apr√©s-swim - are simply not to be missed. Truly an experience that lives long in the memory!

Date & Time


RGM 2023 will take place on Friday, June 30th Check-in from 09:00 at Baltimore harbour. The ferry to Sherkin Island will depart at 10:30. We aim to begin the swim at 11:00.

Safety & Fundraising


Saftey is paramount at RGM and your Registration includes full event insurance and a chaperone from Baltimore's RNLI. Local volunteers will also kayak alongside swimmers in teams, ensuring evey swimmer is monitored and cared for. If anyone is in any difficulty, we'll get you out of the water quickly! Always remember to FLOAT! should you find yourself starting to tire!


Your welcome pack will include your personal sponsership link - we encourage and hope all participants will fundraise as much as they can. All fundraising from Richie's Golden Mile is donated to Temple Street Childrens Hospital - so please support this worthy cause as much as you can!
