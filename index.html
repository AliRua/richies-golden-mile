Richie's Golden Mile


RGM is an annual open-water swim from Sherkin Island to Baltimore harbour. Covering a distance of one nautical mile, it is the perfect swim-meet for your calander whether your just beginning your open water adventure or are a seasoned triathlete looking to rack up your practice.

Location


Situated on the Teddy Bears toes of Ireland in Baltimore, West Cork, you'll find this is one of the most beautiful and idyllic settings for a swim along the Wild Atlantic Way. Hosted late into the balmy month of June against the backdrop of this cosy fishing village, the swim itself - and of course the apr√©s-swim - are simply not to be missed. Truly an experience that lives long in the memory!

Safety


Saftey is paramount at RGM and your Registration includes full event insurance and a chaperone from Baltimore's RNLI. Local volunteers will also kayak alongside swimmers in teams, ensuring evey swimmer is monitored and cared for. If anyone is in any difficulty, we'll get you out of the water quickly.
