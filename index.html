Richie's Golden Mile


RGM is an annual open-water swim from Sherkin Island to Baltimore harbour. Covering a distance of one nautical mile, it is the perfect swim-meet for your calander whether your just beginning your open water adventure or are a seasoned triathlete looking to rack up your practice.
